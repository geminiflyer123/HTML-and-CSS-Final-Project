Mayor Joaquin Bothwick is the son of Renaud Bothwick, who famously won the title of Auchburly in a bet with the great-grandson of it's founder, Sighard Auch VI. When he isn't busy catching up on social media, Mayor Bothwick works tirelessly to improve trade relations with neighbors.

In his spare time, he likes long walks on the beach, chocolate malt milkshakes and turning out peasants' pockets.
