Our Mission


The mission of our city is to foster a free and open trade, which attracts and retains skilled artisans of all varieties. Our business is prosperity, and as such, war and conflict are to be discouraged. Surely through commerce, we can also forge an era of peace and prosperity for all.

Because we are a commercial city, we will never actively seek wartime conflict. That being said, we will also spend an appropriate portion of our resources on suitable defense. We are a loyal vassal and will work tirelessly to promote the interests of our county Paramisa, our duchy, Zylphania, and our kingdom, Alesia. May the gods save our glorious King, Phyllain of Xeilias!
